FAQ

Do I need a WhatsApp Business API account?
+

We handle the entire API setup for you — it's included in every plan. We'll guide you through the Meta Business verification process and get your number approved. Most clients are approved within 48 hours.

How long does the setup take?
+

3 to 5 business days from the kickoff call to your AI going live. We move fast — we've done this enough times to have a streamlined process. Complex enterprise deployments may take up to 2 weeks.

What if something breaks after launch?
+

We're on it before you even notice. Monthly retainer clients get proactive monitoring + same-day fixes. We treat your automation like it's our own business — because if your bot breaks, our reputation breaks.

Do you work with businesses globally?
+

Yes. WhatsApp Business API works in 180+ countries. We've deployed systems across multiple regions. As long as WhatsApp is available in your market, we can build for you.
